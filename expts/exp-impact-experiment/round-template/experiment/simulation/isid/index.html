Effect of infill density on impact strength.

Objective:

To determine the effect of infill density on impact strength.


Apparatus used:

3D printer, izod test apparatus etc.

 Description Impact strength is defined as its capability to resist a sudden applied load or force. It is usually tested using an Izod test or a Charpy test. Here, izod testing will be used.

Infill Density is the amount of material filled inside the shells of the specimen. It is denoted in percentage value.
